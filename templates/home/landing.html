Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our bootcamp program

Our curriculum covers modern web development technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js, and database management with SQL. You'll also learn version control with Git and deployment strategies.

No prior programming experience is required! Our bootcamp is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to advanced concepts, ensuring everyone can follow along regardless of their background.

The program is designed to be flexible, requiring approximately 20-25 hours per week. This includes live sessions, self-paced learning, and project work. You can study during evenings and weekends to fit your schedule.

You'll have access to experienced instructors, peer mentors, study groups, and a dedicated support team. We also provide career services including resume review, interview preparation, and job placement assistance.

Graduates receive ongoing support after completion, including access to our alumni community, networking events, and continued mentorship. We also provide lifetime access to updated course materials and our professional network.
